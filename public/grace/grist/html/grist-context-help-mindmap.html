1. Select any topic to go to a screen where those questions related to that topic will be displayed.
  2. If you select a topic that requires a higher-level question to be answered first, you will be taken to that higher question, with access to the topic available when you have answered it.
  3. If you select any of the individual risk names (e.g. suicide), it will let you answer the risk specific questions followed by the general questions relating to all risks, but in the context of the suicide risk.
    1. Some questions, such as personality, have different influences on different risks.
    2. The influence will be correctly shown for the risk chosen.
    3. All answers to general questions will be shared across all risks, so you only need to provide them once.
  4. If you select the "risks" topic, it will provide you with the sequential interface (option number 5 on the Home Page) where all the risks and questions can be answered.
  5. The buttons on the left-hand side of the panel above the mind map take you to the stages after you have collected data:
    1. 'Risk Judgements' where you can provide one or more risk evaluations along with comments and safety plans.
    2. 'Risk formulation' linking all risk assessments to overall management.
    3. 'Safety Plan' where you can provide the overall management plan.
    4. See the 'GRiST process' white-text link at the very top right-hand part of the screen for more information about these options.
